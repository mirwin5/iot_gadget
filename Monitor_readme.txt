Notes on the Sewer monitor modification

====== Digital In/Out ======
- Valve 1 Sewer
1. D out Open valve relay  Pin
2. D out Close valve relay Pin

- Valve 2 Water
1. D out Open valve relay  Pin
2. D out Close valve relay Pin

- Level Sensor
1. Analog in Pin

- High Level Sensor
1. D in Magnetic Float switch Pin
