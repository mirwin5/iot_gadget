Notes on the Sewer monitor modification

====== Digital In/Out ======
- Valve 1 Sewer
1. D out Open valve relay  Pin IO35
2. D out Close valve relay Pin IO34

- Valve 2 Water
1. D out Open valve relay  Pin IO33
2. D out Close valve relay Pin IO32

- Level Sensor
1. Analog in Pin IO25

- High Level Sensor
1. D in Magnetic Float switch Pin IO25
